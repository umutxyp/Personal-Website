## Recycling the Future

DNZ Geri Dönüşüm is an environmentally conscious recycling company with modern facilities in Istanbul and Kocaeli. I develop and maintain its corporate website, lead forms, and internal dashboards.

### Our Mission

Deliver sustainable recycling for metal, cable, electronics, and vehicle scrap while protecting natural resources through modern processes.

### Services

- Metal, copper, steel, iron & aluminum recycling
- Cable and electronic scrap separation
- Vehicle scrap dismantling and evaluation
- Fast scrap sales, appraisal, and consulting

### Highlights

- 24/7 support lines and instant pricing tools
- On-site appraisal, logistics planning and reporting
- Most up-to-date scrap price data and automatic market comparisons

### Stack & Responsibilities

Next.js · React · Tailwind CSS · Node.js · MongoDB · Resend · Map integrations

- Built bilingual landing pages with SEO-rich copy and conversion tracking.
- Implemented lead pipelines that sync customer requests with internal ops tools.
- Added sustainability dashboards that visualize tonnage, savings, and compliance metrics.
