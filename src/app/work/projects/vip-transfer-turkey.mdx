## Welcome to Vip Transfer Turkey

I own the complete web experience for Vip Transfer Turkey, a company that delivers airport, inter-city, and corporate transfers for thousands of guests each month. The website processes bookings exceeding €1M monthly revenue.

### Services

- Airport transfers with meet-and-greet services
- Intra-city and inter-city private rides
- Corporate car rentals with on-demand fleets
- Business and leisure transfers tailored to each guest

### Why Choose Vip Transfer Turkey?

- **Safety First:** Fleet maintenance schedules, telemetry monitoring, and professional drivers ensure secure trips.
- **Comfortable Travel:** Luxury vehicles, climate control, Wi-Fi, and concierge-level touch points.
- **Personalized Service:** Every booking is customized, from VIP meetups to business itineraries.
- **On-Time Delivery:** Dispatch automation and driver apps ensure guests never miss a flight or meeting.

### Stack & Responsibilities

Next.js · React · Tailwind CSS · Node.js · PostgreSQL · Stripe · Multilingual CMS

- Rebuilt the booking funnel with SEO-first pathways and instant pricing.
- Integrated multi-language content (TR, EN, DE, RU) and currency-aware payments.
- Added live fleet dashboards, CRM integrations, and automated email/SMS flows.
